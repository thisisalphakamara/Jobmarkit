# Hero Background Image Guide

## Image Requirements

### File Details

- **Filename**: `hero-bg-sierra-leone.jpg`
- **Location**: `client/public/hero-bg-sierra-leone.jpg`
- **Format**: JPG or WebP (recommended for better compression)
- **Dimensions**: 1920x1080px minimum (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **File Size**: Under 500KB for optimal loading speed

### Image Content Specifications

#### Primary Subject

- **Sierra Leonean professional** (preferably a young adult, 20-35 years old)
- **Using a laptop or desktop computer**
- **Applying for jobs online** (visible job portal/website on screen)
- **Professional attire** (business casual or formal wear)
- **Confident, focused expression**

#### Composition Guidelines

- **Subject positioned on the left or center-left** of the frame
- **Computer screen visible** with job-related content
- **Good lighting** - natural or soft artificial light
- **Background**: Modern office, home office, or co-working space
- **Depth of field**: Subject in focus, background slightly blurred

#### Visual Style

- **High quality, professional photography**
- **Warm, inviting color tones**
- **Good contrast** for text overlay readability
- **Not too busy** - should not compete with text content
- **Modern, clean aesthetic**

### Alternative Image Options

If you can't find the exact image described above, consider these alternatives:

1. **Sierra Leonean professional in business setting**
2. **Young person using smartphone/tablet for job search**
3. **Group of Sierra Leonean professionals collaborating**
4. **Modern office environment with Sierra Leonean workers**

### Image Sources

#### Free Stock Photo Websites

- **Unsplash**: Search for "African professional", "job search", "computer work"
- **Pexels**: Search for "business person", "office work", "job application"
- **Pixabay**: Search for "professional", "computer", "business"

#### Paid Stock Photo Websites

- **Shutterstock**: High-quality professional images
- **Adobe Stock**: Premium stock photography
- **iStock**: Professional business photography

#### Local Photography

- **Hire a local photographer** in Sierra Leone
- **Use authentic, local imagery** for better connection
- **Ensure proper model releases** if using identifiable people

### Technical Implementation

The image is already implemented in the Hero component with:

- **Responsive design** - scales properly on all devices
- **Fallback gradient** - shows if image fails to load
- **Smooth transitions** - fades in when loaded
- **Overlay for readability** - ensures text remains visible
- **Optimized loading** - progressive image loading

### Image Optimization Tips

1. **Compress the image** to reduce file size while maintaining quality
2. **Use WebP format** if possible for better compression
3. **Test on different devices** to ensure good appearance
4. **Consider multiple sizes** for different screen resolutions
5. **Add proper alt text** for accessibility

- "Job search computer"

- "Business person laptop"

### Important Notes

- **Ensure the image represents diversity** and inclusivity
- **Avoid stereotypes** or clichéd representations
- **Make sure the image aligns** with your brand values
- **Test the image** with your text overlay to ensure readability
- **Consider cultural sensitivity** in image selection

The image should make users feel confident and inspired to use your job portal, while clearly representing the Sierra Leonean community you serve.
